Bonjour,
I'm Karolline,
Web designer.

In 2017, I graduated in Marketing at the Catholic University of Petrópolis in Brazil and worked in a public office of the Brazilian government as an administrative assistant until I came to study Web design and development at CDI College in Montreal, Canada. In that course, I nurtured my desire to learn more about technology and design, a subject that I have been interested in since I was a child.

When I arrived in Montreal, I dedicated myself to studies, and I worked on my own as social media content manager and I developed some websites through WordPress and Shopify for some small businesses. In this way, I have already experienced working in a group and alone, and I was able to do both easily.

When I am not studying or working, I enjoy playing video games, watching movies and TV series, and I am also completely passionate about nature, traveling and adventures.
